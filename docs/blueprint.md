# **App Name**: SportSphere

## Core Features:

- Listing Creation & Management: Users can create, edit, and manage listings across various sports categories including Coaches, Venues, Equipment, Events, Teams, Training Programs, Fitness Trainers, Physiotherapists, Nutritionists, Sports Transport, Accommodation, and Repairs.
- Listing Search & Discovery: Powerful search and filtering tools allow users to find relevant sports-related services and products based on categories, location, availability, and keywords.
- User Profiles & Authentication: Secure user registration and login, with customizable profiles for both service providers and consumers to manage their activities and listings.
- In-App Messaging: Enable direct, secure communication between users to discuss listings, inquiries, and booking details.
- Booking & Inquiry System: A streamlined process for users to send booking requests or inquiries directly through the platform, and for providers to respond and manage them.
- Personalized Listing Recommendations: An AI-powered tool that suggests relevant coaches, venues, or events to users based on their profile, search history, and inferred interests.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A dynamic and spirited deep blue (#1755CF), reflecting athleticism and community. This color is saturated and vibrant to provide clear contrast against lighter backgrounds.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, blue-gray (#F2F5F8), subtly echoing the primary hue while maintaining a clean, open feel.
- Accent color: A vibrant violet (#824EDE) provides a distinct but harmonious contrast to the primary blue, used for highlighting interactive elements and calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a sans-serif font chosen for its modern, techy feel, perfect for bold titles and headings that evoke an energetic sports atmosphere.
- Body font: 'Inter', a neutral and highly readable sans-serif, ensuring clarity and objective presentation of listing details and general text content.
- Utilize modern, clean line icons that are easily recognizable and sports-themed, providing intuitive navigation and representing various listing categories.
- A clean, modular layout with a strong emphasis on content hierarchy, ensuring easy readability and navigation across all devices, with dedicated sections for listings, profiles, and messages.
